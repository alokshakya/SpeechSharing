Welcome to the central speech sharing platform where you can add, edit, delete and share your speech.

Features

View all your speeches saved in the system. You can see your speeches and edit them, delete them and share.

Add a speech to the system. For this you have to enter speech text, author name, keywords of the speech.

The ability to search the speeches. You will be able to search through author name, speech keywords, speech date
